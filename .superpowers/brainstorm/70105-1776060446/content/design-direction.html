A. Faithful Port

Keep the exact Bayaan aesthetic — warm parchment light (#f4f3ec), deep navy dark (#050b10), Manrope font, purple/green accents. The web feels like the same app on a bigger screen. Strong brand consistency.

B. Spotify-Dark First

Dark-first UI inspired by Spotify's layout language — bold cards, persistent bottom player bar, sidebar navigation, vibrant accent on dark backgrounds. The listening experience drives the design. Light mode available but secondary.

C. Hybrid — Bayaan DNA + Spotify UX

Keep Bayaan's brand colors and warm personality, but adopt Spotify's navigation patterns (sidebar, persistent player, card-based browsing). Dark-first with Bayaan's navy (#050b10), reading mode gets its own clean light theme. Best of both worlds.
